4.  将`0FFFF0~0FFFFFH`中的8个字，逆序复制到`00200H\~0020FH`中

运行下面的指令即可解决该题

```assembly
assume cs:codesg
codesg segment
mov ax,20H
mov ds,ax
mov ax,0FFFFH
mov ss,ax
mov sp,0
pop [E]
pop [C]
pop [A]
pop [8]
pop [6]
pop [4]
pop [2]
pop [0]
mov ax,4c00H
int 21H
codesg ends
end
```
